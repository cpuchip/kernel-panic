Everything that walks the kitchen, popped open. Designed by Michael's son and built into the game. Butter is what you earn per pop; D is how many lives you lose if a kernel reaches the bowl — and a leak is instant game over if it's big enough, so don't let the cobs through. Bigger enemies pop into smaller ones, just like Bloons.

The round system

100 rounds, then endless Free Play. Boss rounds are fixed; everything in between ramps up smoothly.

How rounds flow: new kernel types phase in as the round climbs — rush kernels, then the immunity colours, then the tanky ceramic-tier — and the counts grow. The four big cobs are pinned to milestone rounds so you always know when a boss is coming.
